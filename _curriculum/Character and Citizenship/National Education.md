<p>National Education (NE) comprises a wide range of school efforts that
help students experience and explore their national identity as Singaporeans,
understand the realities and challenges unique to our nation, and foster
a sense of hope and belonging to Singapore. These efforts engender the
will to act and mould our Mayflowerians to be active contributors to the
community in Singapore and the world.</p>
<p>NE in Mayflower aims to be empowering and aspirational, allowing for individual
sense-making to nurture engaged citizens who are socially aware, adept
at critical thinking, and informed about local, regional, and global issues.</p>
