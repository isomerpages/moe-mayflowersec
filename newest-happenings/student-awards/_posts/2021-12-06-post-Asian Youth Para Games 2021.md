Mayflower Secondary School is proud that our student Colin Soon has won his first two gold medals at the Asian Youth Para Games 2021 in Bahrain on Saturday, 4 December 2021. He claimed two bronze medals in the S11-13 100m backstroke and freestyle events previously in Dubai 2017.
