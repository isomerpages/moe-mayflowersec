<p>Dear Parents/Guardians</p>
<p>The 2025 O-Level results will be released on <strong>Wednesday, 14 Jan at 2pm</strong>.
While we await the results, let us also remember to celebrate their efforts,
regardless of the results. We can show support for our teens by showing
them through our words and actions that their grades do not define who
they are.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p>Your support to your teen during this milestone is important. Use this
opportunity to have open conversations and guide them as they consider
their next steps.&nbsp;</p>

<h3><strong>ECG Counselling Support</strong></h3>
<p>For ECG counselling support, students may contact the ECG Counsellor in
their schools or make an ECG counselling appointment with the ECG Centre
@ MOE from 7 - 21&nbsp;January 2026 (Weekdays: 9.00 am to 5.00 pm; Saturdays:
9.00 am to 12.00 pm) through the appointment booking form below.</p>
<p>Booking Form for ECG Counselling Appointments at the ECG Centre @ MOE:
<a href="https://go.gov.sg/moe-ecg-centre" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/moe-ecg-centre.</a>
</p>
<p>Please note the following</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Reporting centre is the school HALL.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You need to be in SCHOOL ATTIRE &amp; BE PROPERLY GROOMED.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Your hair must NOT be highlighted or dyed. Boys must NOT have long hair.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Those in improper attire / or poor grooming will be turned away and may
not be allowed to collect their results. They may collect their result
slips the following day.</p>
