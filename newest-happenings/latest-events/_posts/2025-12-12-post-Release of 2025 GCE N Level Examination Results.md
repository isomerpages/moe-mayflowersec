<p>The results of the 2025 Singapore-Cambridge General Certificate of Education
Normal (Academic)-[N(A)] and Normal (Technical)-[N(T)] Level Examinations
will be released on Thursday, 18 December 2025, 2.00pm.</p>
<p>Arrangements have been made for school candidates to receive their results
from the secondary school in the hall from 2.00pm on Thursday, 18 December
2025. School candidates who are unwell should seek medical attention and
not return to school to collect their results.</p>
<p>Please note the following;</p>
<p>(a)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Reporting centre is the school <strong><u>CANTEEN</u></strong>.</p>
<p>(b)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; You need
to be in <strong><u>SCHOOL ATTIRE &amp; BE PROPERLY GROOMED</u></strong>.</p>
<p>(c)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Your hair must <strong><u>NOT</u></strong> be highlighted or dyed. Boys must <strong><u>NOT</u></strong> have
long hair.</p>
<p>Those in improper attire / or poor grooming will be <strong>turned away</strong> and <strong>may not be allowed</strong> to
collect their results. They may collect their result slips the following
day.</p>
<p>School candidates who are unable to collect their results in person can
also appoint a proxy to collect a physical copy of their results from the
school. Proxies are required to produce the relevant documents for the
schoolâ€™s verification when collecting the results. School candidates may
contact their school for assistance with the appointment of a proxy.</p>
