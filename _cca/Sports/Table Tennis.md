<p>The Table Tennis CCA aims to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>improve table tennis skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>develop an ever-lasting love for the sport</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>inculcate values that will go beyond just the sporting arena</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Table tennis is more than just a game played across a net — it is a sport
that requires focus, speed and strategy. At MFSS, the Table Tennis Team
is united by a shared passion for the game.</p>
<p>Through regular and rigorous training sessions, as well as bonding opportunities,
members grow in confidence, commitment and teamwork. They develop not only
technical skills but also a strong sense of commitment to the CCA. Table
tennis provides a supportive environment where students can develop both
as players and as individuals.</p>
