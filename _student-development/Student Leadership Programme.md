<h3>Vision</h3>
<blockquote>
<p>Caring and Supportive Culture where Peers Strengthen One Another</p>
</blockquote>
<h3>Motto</h3>
<blockquote>
<p>To Serve, To Lead, To Grow</p>
</blockquote>
<p>At Mayflower Secondary School, our Student Leadership Programme serves
as a platform to develop the generous spirit among our Mayflowerians. We
deepen the inculcation of respect by celebrating differences and contributing
to society with empathy. Our student leaders embody our core principles:
To Serve with Commitment, To Lead with Moral Courage and Empathy, and To
Grow with Open-Mindedness.</p>
<p>We believe that every Mayflowerian is a leader who deserves the opportunity
to develop their leadership qualities. Therefore, leadership opportunities
are provided to all students at various levels, ensuring inclusive participation
in building our caring school community.</p>
<h2>Our Student Leadership Framework</h2>
<p>Inspired by our shared vision, every group of student leaders in Mayflower
plays a unique role in their respective leadership positions. Our student
leaders demonstrate <strong>courage</strong> to do right, the <strong>mindset to learn and serve</strong>,
and the heart to <strong>lead by example</strong> with <strong>pride</strong> for
school and nation. They practice servant leadership and peer leadership
through five key practices: Model the Way, Inspire a Shared Vision, Challenge
the Process, Enable Others to Act, and Encourage the Heart.</p>

<p>Through comprehensive training platforms, mentoring opportunities, and
real-world leadership experiences, our student leaders develop essential
skills including design thinking, active listening, conflict resolution,
team building, and strategic visioning.</p>
<p>Our Student Leadership Programme ensures that every Mayflowerian has the
opportunity to serve their community, lead with purpose, and grow into
confident, caring leaders ready to make positive differences in school
and society.</p>
